War Plan Reference Guide

Optimized paths & breakpoints for Diablo 4: Lord of Hatred

The Golden Rules

  • Rush XP Progression: Path through nodes to maximize War Plan tree XP. Ignore the raw node rewards—they pale in comparison to actual activity scaling, unless you hit a rare Mythic-tier reward.
  • Reroll Bad Layouts: If a layout forces you into inefficient paths or layout-mandatory blocks, scrap it and generate a new plan.
  • Skip Infernal Hordes: Do not run them. They consume too much time for negligible progression value this season. Avoid entirely.
Activity Breakpoints & Priorities
1. Bosses (5 Points Minimum)
Absolute Top Priority
Why: Ultimate wealth and gearing engine. Spamming bosses without these nodes is massive resource griefing.
  • Nemesis Lairs (1-3 pts): Unlocks layered boss maps that spawn consecutive targets for massive high-quality loot streams and elevated Mythic Seal drops.
  • Lair of Plenty (Next 2 pts): Directly doubles the loot dropped on every single boss kill.
2. Pits (4 Points Minimum)
Essential Progression
Why: Drastically cuts down glyph leveling times. These 4 points easily triple or quadruple your efficiency.
  • Current Haste & Curt's Blessing: Provides multiple bonus glyph upgrade attempts at the end of each run.
  • Heart of the Stone & Damn Thieves: Causes massive progression orbs to drop, effectively cutting map completion times in half.
3. Kurast Undercity (4 Points Minimum)
Target Farming
Why: The premier location to targeted-farm specific items like Mythics, Talismans, and Charms.
  • Gutterfilth (3 pts): Activates blue beacons that spawn Goblins. Critical for multiplying rare tribute run loot (potential for up to 4 Mythics per run).
  • Initiative (1 pt): Straight multiplier to overall baseline loot quantity.
Group Play Strategy: In a party, only the player hitting the blue beacons needs Gutterfilth. The player opening the actual tribute box only needs Initiative. Rotate roles to maximize points.
4. Helltides (3 to 6 Points)
Target Leveling
Why: Unmatched speeds for target character leveling.
  • Ramrod: Spawns maggots that drop hyper-dense XP orbs. The structural foundation of the Helltide strategy.
  • Meat Brothers: Spawns Butchers that drop Profane Mindcages. Stack these up to 3x to skyrocket monster level and XP yields.
Respect Optimization: Once you safely stockpile a deep stack of Mindcages (20–40), respect out of Meat Brothers and shift those points into monster density or power nodes for raw XP.
5. Nightmare Dungeons (3 Points)
Material Farming
Why: Generally low impact, but mandatory for harvesting materials required to roll the Vali.
  • Fearless Conviction + Nemesis: Amplifies elite pack sizes and forces significantly higher Primordial Dust drops per elite kill.
6. Tree of Whispers
Passive Filler
Why: Passively ticks up in the background. The actual choices here offer minimal scaling, so treat them as pure layout filler to bridge paths.
