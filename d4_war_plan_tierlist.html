  • Spawns Maggots + Pang of Duriel from Hellwyrm ambushes

Kurast Undercity

Fast runs, customizable rewards via Tributes. All 3 paths viable.

Left or Middle
  • Left: Better end-of-run loot + Forgotten Souls on completion
  • Middle: Unlocks Ancestral Armament tribute drops
  • Right: More XP and Tributes — good if grinding levels
